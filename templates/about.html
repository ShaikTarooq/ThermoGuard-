About ThermoGuard

Built to keep critical spaces
at a safe temperature, automatically.

ThermoGuard started as a simple question: why do server rooms, labs, and storage facilities still rely on someone noticing it's gotten too hot? We built the system that notices first.

Our mission

Heat-related downtime is preventable. ThermoGuard exists to give facilities — from a single server closet to a multi-zone industrial site — a always-on layer of thermal awareness that reacts in seconds, not after someone walks past with a thermometer.

Every reading is logged, every fan and AC action is recorded, and every threshold is yours to tune. We built the audit trail first, because trust in an automated system starts with being able to check its work.

How we think about reliability

01

Hysteresis by default

Our control logic never flips a relay twice for the same temperature swing. ON and OFF thresholds are deliberately separated to protect hardware longevity.

02

Everything is logged

Every reading, every fan/AC transition, every critical event — stored, searchable, and exportable. Nothing about the system's behavior is a black box.

03

Configurable, not fixed

Thresholds live in Settings, not in code. Different rooms run hotter or colder than spec sheets assume — your system should adapt to that.
