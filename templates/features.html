Features

Everything you need to monitor
and control thermal risk.

From live telemetry to exportable audit trails — built as one connected system, not a pile of separate tools.

Automatic fan & AC control

Hysteresis-based logic switches cooling on and off at separate thresholds, preventing rapid relay cycling while keeping temperature inside a safe band.

Live analytics dashboard

Average and peak temperature, humidity trends, and fan/AC duty cycle — broken down across 1-hour to 30-day windows, charted with Chart.js.

Searchable history

Filter every logged reading by sensor, status level, or date range. Built for the moment someone asks "what happened last Tuesday at 3pm?"

One-click CSV export

Export exactly the filtered view you're looking at — for compliance reports, incident reviews, or sharing with a facilities team.

Tunable thresholds

Set fan ON/OFF and AC ON/OFF temperatures per deployment, with built-in validation so your thresholds can't be configured into a flapping mess.

Responsive, anywhere

The full dashboard, analytics, and history views adapt cleanly from a wide ops monitor down to a phone screen during an on-call check.
